This guide provides presales leaders with a framework for evaluating AI tools by starting with clear problem definition and applying a three-pillar assessment covering capability fit, operational impact, and adoption potential. The article emphasizes measuring tangible outcomes and involving teams early rather than relying on vendor hype.
