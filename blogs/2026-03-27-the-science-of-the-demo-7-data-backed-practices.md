This report analyzes survey data from 300+ sales and presales professionals to identify seven evidence-based practices that distinguish closing demos from unsuccessful ones. Key findings emphasize storytelling over features, keeping demos brief, leveraging AI for backend work, and measuring outcomes rather than activity metrics.
